Voice workflow
Open-loop safety: The payload forces ChatGPT to ask: “Where are you now, what are you doing, and what do you see?” before coaching. It should then give one instruction, wait, and confirm.
How to use with ChatGPT voice
  1. Click Build & Open ChatGPT. The payload is copied to your clipboard.
  2. Paste into ChatGPT and send.
  3. On desktop web, click the Voice icon on the right side of the prompt window and allow microphone access.
  4. On mobile, tap the Voice icon near the message bar. You can mute/unmute with the microphone icon.
  5. Say what step you are on. The first expected phrase is: I am at the bedside with the probe in hand...
